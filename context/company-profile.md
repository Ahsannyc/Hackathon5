## Pain Points Driving AI Employee Need

### Current Challenges (Why This Matters Now)

1. **Response Time Too Slow**
   - Customers wait 6-12 hours for answers
   - WhatsApp customers expect <5 min (we deliver 15-30 min when available)
   - Competitors respond in 4-8 hours; we need <2 min

2. **24/7 Coverage Gap**
   - No support outside 9-5 PT (customer base is global)
   - 2-3am requests from EU/APAC go unanswered
   - Users frustrated when immediate help unavailable

3. **Repetitive Questions (60% of Tickets)**
   - "How do I set up task dependencies?"
   - "Where are my reports?"
   - "How do I reset my password?"
   - CS team drowning in FAQ-level questions instead of solving complex issues

4. **Scaling Bottleneck**
   - Growing 20% MoM but support team fixed at 2 FTEs
   - Can't hire CS agents faster than customer growth
   - Would need 4 FTEs by end of 2026 at current growth rate
   - Cost: $400k/year in salaries + benefits

5. **Multi-Timezone Complexity**
   - Customers in 20+ countries (8 hours of time zone spread)
   - Impossible to provide 24/7 coverage with human team alone
   - Morning hours for one region = midnight for another

TOTAL: $270k/year
